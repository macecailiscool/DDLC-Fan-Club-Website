DDLC Fan Club - Rules

  1. Respect and Kindness: Treat all members with respect and kindness. Be inclusive, understanding, and considerate of others' opinions and feelings.
  2. No Spoilers: Avoid sharing spoilers or discussing major plot points without appropriate spoiler warnings. Respect others who may not have completed the game or experienced certain aspects of it yet.
  3. Positive Environment: Foster a positive and supportive environment for all members. Avoid engaging in any form of bullying, harassment, or discrimination.
  4. Appropriate Language: Use appropriate language and refrain from using offensive, vulgar, or derogatory words or phrases. Maintain a friendly and welcoming atmosphere for everyone.
  5. Respect Privacy: Respect the privacy of fellow members. Do not share personal information about others without their consent.
  6. Constructive Discussions: Engage in thoughtful and constructive discussions related to Doki Doki Literature Club. Express your opinions respectfully and be open to different perspectives.
  7. Compliance with School Rules: Adhere to the rules and regulations of Cedar Bluff School while participating in the DDLC Fan Club.
  8. Club Attendance: Regularly attend club meetings and participate actively. Notify the club organizer in advance if unable to attend a meeting.
  9. Club Events and Activities: Show enthusiasm and actively participate in club events, activities, and projects. Contribute ideas and help create a vibrant club experience for all members.
