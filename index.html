About DDLC Fan Club

Welcome to the DDLC Fan Club, hosted by Mace Cail, at Cedar Bluff School! Our club is dedicated to all things related to the game "Doki Doki Literature Club" and its incredible developer team, Team Salvato.

Doki Doki Literature Club is a visual novel that takes you on a rollercoaster ride of emotions. Join us to dive deeper into the captivating story, intriguing characters, and thought-provoking themes presented in the game. Whether you're a fan of the literature club members, Monika, Yuri, Natsuki, and Sayori, or you want to discuss the game's hidden secrets and unsettling twists, this is the perfect place for you.

As a member of the DDLC Fan Club, you'll have the opportunity to engage in stimulating discussions, share fan art, fanfiction, theories, and explore the various mods and adaptations created by the talented DDLC community. We organize regular meetings, game nights, and special events where you can connect with fellow enthusiasts and celebrate the game's impact on our lives.

Join us on this incredible journey and unravel the mysteries of Doki Doki Literature Club together. Don't miss out on the chance to meet like-minded individuals who share your passion for this groundbreaking game!
